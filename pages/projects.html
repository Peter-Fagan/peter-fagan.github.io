PROJECTS

Here are some of the projects I have worked on

Pacman clock

This was done purely in CSS, I wanted to see what I could do just with CSS shapes




AmIRight

This was a group project done with Tomas Haffenden. Using Ruby and Backbone we created a single page polling app. The user creates a question with 2-4 answers which are then presented in a graph

Character Generator

Inspired by my years of playing Dungeons and Dragons, I created a character generator. I used Ruby on Rails construct the app and then deployed it with Heroku.


3D Maze generator

This was my final project, to create a randomly generated maze, render it and 3D and make it navigatable by the user. The maze was generated with Javascript and I used Babylon.js to render the world and move the camera
